Hybrids ¬£60

Single and fanned lash extensions are attached to natural lashes. Hybrid lash extensions give a full glam look. Great for those who want more than natural, but less than dramatic.

Russians ¬£70

Infills ¬£35

Length of time: 2 hours.
Multiple lash extensions are attached to natural lashes. Russian Volume lash extensions have the most full and dramatic look. Great for sparse lashes and those who want to stand out.

Removal ¬£10

Length of time: 30mins.
Get those extensions removed safely without damaging your natural lashes. Can be booked alongside a full set/lash lift/lash tint.


Lift and tint ¬£30

Length of time: 1 hour 30mins.
A perfect treatment for my natural queens, includes a perm of the natural lashes to appear darker and curly. Mascara look without the mascara! Lasts up to 6 weeks.

Henna brows ¬£35

Length of time: 1 hour 30mins.
Henna brows are a type of tinting that stains the skin and hairs giving you a fuller, shaped brow. Complimentary wax included. Effects can last up to 4 weeks. Perfect for sparse natural brows.

Hybrid brows ¬£25

Length of time: 1 hour
The perfect add-on to any lash treatment, a blend between tinted and henna brows colour-matched to your tones. Colour lasts on the skin for up to a week and on the hairs for up to 6 weeks. Patch test required for new hybrid brow clients.
